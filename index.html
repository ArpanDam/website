Brief Bio

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, advised by Dr. Bivas Mitra. My research focuses on social network analysis, influence maximization, fairness-aware recommendation systems, and graph representation learning.

Previously, I completed my Master’s degree at IIIT Vadodara under the supervision of Dr. Ashish Phophalia. I am currently a member of the Complex Networks Research Group at IIT Kharagpur.

Research Interests

  • Social Network Analysis
  • Influence Maximization
  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Fairness in Machine Learning
  • Recommender Systems
